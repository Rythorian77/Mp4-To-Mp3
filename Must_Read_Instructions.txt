You will find the folder "Items" above. Open it and copy the "bin" folder.
Now go to your projects "bin" folder and open it. Next, open the "Debug" folder.
Last: Paste the bin folder you copied from the "items" folder in there.

The reason for this is that there are 3 exe files that are needed to be used:
FILES:
*ffmpeg.exe
*ffplay.exe
*ffprobe.exe

Your project will not function without them.
